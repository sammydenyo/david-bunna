Who am i?

Creating this website i didnt know what to write here so i decided to fix somthing eventually. This website is just describing who i am and where i am heading in Life. I know i want to change the world my own way the best way i can but growing up it wasn't easy as easy coming from humble beginnings to youthful mistakes, I have been able to define my purpose which is to make Bunna Empire the greatest ever entertainment company out of Africa with the dreams made out of my head.
The full story of how bunna empire started will be in my first book so you guys will have to wait for that but you already know i posses a strong passion for Arts, Music, Innovation and Entertainment which is the foundation of the whole idea of Bunna Empire.
My passion for everyday learning and innovation drove me to study music business at SAE Institute and COPASA Cape Town and have so far developed a broad network of creative and top industry contacts across top African countries including Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Kenya, Sierra Leone, Liberia, Uganda, Morrocco and established markets like the United States and the UK.
Currently a backpacker and can be anywhere at anytime, as i am growing my company Bunna Empire and also touring the world networking and making the right link ups and connections, doing what i can while i can so feel free to hit me up lets collaborate on something
